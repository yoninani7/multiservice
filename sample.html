    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find quick answers to common questions about our services and policies.

    What are your opening hours?

    We're open Monday to Saturday from 8:30 AM to 7:30 PM, and on Sundays from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We're also open on most public holidays with reduced hours.

    Do you offer multilingual assistance?

    Yes! Our staff speaks Italian, English, Arabic, Amharic, and French. We can assist with forms, translations, and explanations in these languages.

    How do I book the Office Pod?

    You can book through our website, by calling us, or visiting in person. We recommend booking at least 2 hours in advance for guaranteed availability. Same-day bookings are subject to availability.

    Can I cancel or reschedule my Office Pod booking?

    Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 2 hours before your booking without charge. Cancellations within 2 hours may incur a 50% fee. Rescheduling is free if done more than 2 hours in advance.

    Do you accept credit/debit cards?

    Yes, we accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), as well as cash. For money transfer services, cash is usually required for sending, while receiving can be done via bank transfer or cash pickup.
